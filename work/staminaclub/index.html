Why

We all face impossible situations. Where sometimes we are deprived of our own choices. Where we don't know what we are fighting for. Where we feel we are not reaching our full potential. Whether we are an entrepreneur or not. Where we feel like a shadow of our destiny. Where we feel totally out of alignment with our life vision. Without knowing where to start. Without knowing how to transform our life. Without a plan, method or strategy to build our own legend. Our time is limited and the clock is ticking. We are fed up with so-called miracle solutions, psychology and personal development of low quality, without any results. And building our own future is too important to waste our time and let mongols give us simplistic advice and make empty promises. We need a plan. A pragmatic, scientific and reliable method. Real visionary strategies. Experience-based feedback. Great life principles. Powerful, game-changing habits.

How to allow each persone to create their own legend thanks to reliable methods, pragmatic strategies and scientific habits?

How

Equal ambitions, different results. The only difference between those who succeed and those who fail, with equivalent starting resources, is the way of thinking. And the mind is like a muscle. It trains, it learns, it gets hurt, it gets up, it tries again and it succeeds. Contrary to what we are taught since our childhood, talent is not innate. It is developed. But you need to have the keys to it. Secrets that are well kept by those who have them and of inestimable value to those who acquire them.

Secrets to build the vision in which you project yourself. To create your own legend.

After meeting dozens of successful entrepreneurs, interviewing handfuls of them, reading their books, learning and applying their methods I decided to create a vault of these secrets. Called Stamina Memoriae, in other words "Memory of Perseverance".

A safe that will be much more valuable if it is shared, used and accessible everywhere, 24 hours a day.

I turn it into the Stamina Club. The one and only place on Earth to create your own legend. Through successful habits, business methods, vision tools, life plans, discipline keys and life secrets.Man can transform his existence by changing his outlook on the world. There are no deserters in the face of destiny, we are born to fulfill ourselves. Nothing else. We are all entrepreneurs of our lives.

Discover now how I bring it to life.
The final solution.

What

In general, with digital, we have forgotten how important it is to build a real experience, a real storytelling and a total immersion in a universe. Building on the vision of the project and the reason why it exists is the key to ensuring the right message, the right approach and above all the best format.

We have designed a special experience with immersive staging, which makes sure to create a unique atmosphere stimulating the vision of each member. Nothing is left to chance. Everything is designed to immerse club members in their vision and help them achieve it.

A brand platform was designed to deploy the brand to its full potential and focus on the company's vision and values. From there we were able to start materializing the brand and designing the overall experience.

We designed a luxury identity, to support the rarity and value of the secrets being passed down. An identity with the look of a great French luxury brand and the colorful world of Tuscany in the heart of the Quattrocento of Italian renaissance and the history of the Medici. A unique monogram representing perseverance, balance, boldness and discipline.

The Stamina Club is a special experience that is articulated in different offers; free, paid and subscription to access the different resources of the club. Simple and effective formats in charge of transmitting the secrets and methods in the most accurate and transparent way. From the newsletter, to the online academy, through e-books, coaching offers on selection and precise programs & plans to develop your own vision. A platform even more private than other resources, where you can find exclusive content. Effective formats to convey value at its highest level.

A private line of clothing is also part of the experience, bearing the monogram and a few words representing the soul of the brand. A sober and limited collection to affirm and underline the will to create one's own legend.

Only members have access to all these resources and access is through the first step of the club's digital platform.
