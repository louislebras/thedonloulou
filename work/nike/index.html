How can we provide all clubs with the best custom equipment, to help them create a true sense of belonging and stimulate the development of talent?

Client Context

In France, soccer is the most popular sport in the country. Every city, small or big, has its own club. More than a community, it is a real institution. Each club is affiliated to the FFF, a major step to become part of the history of sport in France. In partnership with FFF, Nike brings custom furniture and wants to make this customization accessible to all clubs.

Nike and the FFF through their partnership, want to offer a platform for clubs to access the latest equipment models. It is with the teams of Emakina France that Nike and the FFF choose to collaborate to implement this project.

We help them to

• We help a brand new business model to life
• Lead by experience to performance
• Build a platform for clubs & licensees

Work, implication & role

Once the layout and ergonomics of the platform were defined, it was necessary to bring it to life with content that would really guide clubs in the selection of equipment and guarantee for all, the understanding of the features and possibilities of the experience.

Alongside Emakina's designers, my role was to name titles, sections, buttons and all the elements of all the pages of the platform to optimize the performance of the offers proposed to the clubs. A work of synthesis of interview and research was conducted judiciously in order to rely on the elements of speech and desires of the clubs to propose a personalized experience from start to finish.
