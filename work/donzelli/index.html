Context/Insight

The luxury designer furniture market is facing a huge customer experience issue. The majority of companies operating in this field are either distributors, manufacturers or have the rights to publish designer pieces. They survive solely through their products and the prestige of their products, which they sometimes do not even own, without offering a true 360° brand experience. Few brands on the market embody a true essence beyond the products, and forget to capture the desires and needs of their customers. And these days, relying on a great story does not create a true global experience.

How do you create an experience that goes beyond the products and their story, to allow people to design the environment that looks like them?

Approach
that I've recommended

Donzelli was one of my clients when I was self-employed before joining Emakina. First of all, the idea was to understand the high-end furniture market in order to position the brand on which niche it could be positioned. Depending on its expertise, its heritage and its competitors. To overcome the lack of customer experience on the market, it was necessary to think and design an online experience that is similar to that of a store, but even more advanced. Donzelli has become the first furniture design index in the world to offer all furniture pieces on a single platform, all brands included.

Vitra, Cassina, B&B, Flos, Knoll, BoConcept, Rochebobois ...

We had the opportunity to create different spaces and rooms to project the customer into a universe, adding a selection of products to achieve consistency of the pieces chosen online on the digital experience.

A solution supported by augmented reality to arrange a selection of furniture within a space. Donzelli is also equipped with its experience and expertise in design and architecture by proposing pieces of furniture that match together but also spaces and environments already designed so that in a few clicks you can order your custom or well designed entire interior on a single platform.
